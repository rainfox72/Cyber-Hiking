Glow Philosophy

Current problem: Neon green (#00ff41) glows everywhere constantly → flat, fatiguing, no emphasis hierarchy.

New rule: Glow is earned. Base text uses muted tactical green with no glow. Glow reserved for:

  • Active/current waypoint marker
  • Critical vital alerts (pulsing)
  • Action button on hover/press
  • AI decision highlight
  • Victory/summit moment

Glow intensity scales with danger level. Calm conditions = almost no glow. Storm conditions = more environmental light flicker. Crisis = pulsing danger glow on relevant vitals.

Panel Treatment

Backplate: background: rgba(13, 17, 23, 0.85) — dark enough to read text, translucent enough to see mountain through edges.

Blur: backdrop-filter: blur(8px) on panels — mountain detail softens behind instruments, creating depth separation.

Borders: 1px solid rgba(61, 139, 55, 0.15) — faint tactical green edge. Brightens during alerts.

Crisis escalation: As conditions worsen, panel borders shift color (green → amber → red), backplate opacity can pulse, and edge glow intensifies. The instruments react to the danger they're displaying.
