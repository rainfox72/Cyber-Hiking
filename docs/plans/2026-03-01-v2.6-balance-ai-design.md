## Task 1: Resource Drain Economy

**Problem:** AI only selects set_camp and rest, never eat/drink ‚Äî camp/rest are "free" recovery with no resource cost.

**Changes:**

### `vitalCalculator.ts` ‚Äî set_camp case:
- Consume 1 food ration when camping (`player.food -= 1`)
- If no food available: massive morale penalty (`player.morale -= 20`) instead of blocking the action
- Log entry note when camping without food

### `vitalCalculator.ts` ‚Äî rest case:
- Consume 0.5 water when resting (`player.water -= 0.5`)
- If no water: reduced energy recovery (halved)

### Cold Exposure Multiplier:
- If `bodyTemp < 35` (on 0-100 scale): camp and rest consume **double** food/water
  - Camp: 2 food instead of 1
  - Rest: 1.0 water instead of 0.5

### `NavigationConsole.tsx` ‚Äî Update cost descriptions:
- SET CAMP: "4h day / sleep til dawn | -1 Food, Major recovery"
- REST: "2h | -0.5 Water, Minor recovery"
